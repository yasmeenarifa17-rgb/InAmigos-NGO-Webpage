About InAmigos Foundation

Founded with a vision of creating meaningful social impact, InAmigos Foundation works across education, environmental sustainability, animal welfare, women empowerment and skill development initiatives. Through projects such as Seva, Bachpanshala, Jeev, Udaan, Prakriti and Vikas, the organization continues to inspire positive change and empower communities across India.
